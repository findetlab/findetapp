About Findet

Findet was created to solve the common problem of expense tracking. Instead of manually entering every transaction, Findet intelligently reads your SMS messages from banks and payment providers to automatically categorize and track your expenses.

Our app respects your privacy and security. All data is stored locally on your device and synchronized securely through encrypted cloud storage. We never access or share your personal financial information.
