About FinDet

FinDet was created to solve the common problem of expense tracking while maintaining your complete privacy. Instead of manually entering every transaction, FinDet intelligently reads your SMS and RCS messages from banks and payment providers to automatically categorize and track your expenses - all processed locally on your device.

Built Different: FinDet prioritizes your privacy above all else. We believe financial apps should be transparent about data handling and give you complete control.
