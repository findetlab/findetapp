Privacy Policy for Findet

Effective Date:

Last Updated:

1. Introduction

Welcome to Findet ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Findet (the "App").

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

  • Email Address: Used for account creation and authentication
  • Account Preferences: App settings and user preferences

2.2 Financial Information

  • Transaction Data: Transaction amounts, descriptions, categories, and dates that you manually enter
  • Account Information: Bank account names, credit card names, and wallet names (no actual account numbers)
  • SMS Messages: Transaction-related SMS messages from banks and payment providers for automatic transaction detection

2.3 Device Information

  • Device Identifiers: Device ID for app functionality and analytics
  • App Usage Data: Features used, time spent in app, crash reports
  • Device Storage: Local database for offline functionality

2.4 Automatically Collected Information

  • Analytics Data: App performance, feature usage, and crash reports
  • Log Data: Error logs and diagnostic information

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Core App Functionality

  • Transaction Management: Process and categorize your financial transactions
  • SMS Parsing: Automatically detect and parse transaction information from SMS messages
  • Data Synchronization: Sync your data across devices using your account
  • Expense Tracking: Provide spending analytics and financial insights

3.2 App Improvement

  • Analytics: Understand how users interact with the app to improve features
  • Bug Fixes: Identify and resolve technical issues
  • Performance Optimization: Enhance app speed and reliability

3.3 Communication

  • Account Management: Send important updates about your account or the app
  • Customer Support: Respond to your inquiries and provide assistance

4. How We Store and Protect Your Information

4.1 Data Storage

  • Local Storage: Data is stored locally on your device in an encrypted database
  • Cloud Storage: Data is securely stored in Google Firebase Firestore with encryption in transit and at rest
  • No Third-Party Storage: We do not store your data with any other third-party services

4.2 Security Measures

  • Encryption: All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest
  • Authentication: Secure user authentication through Firebase Auth
  • Access Controls: Strict access controls to your personal data
  • Regular Security Updates: We regularly update our security practices

5. Information Sharing and Disclosure

5.1 We Do Not Sell Your Data

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

5.2 Service Providers

We may share your information with:

  • Google Firebase: For data storage, authentication, and analytics
  • Google Play Services: For app functionality and crash reporting

5.3 Legal Requirements

We may disclose your information if required to do so by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.

6. Your Rights and Choices

6.1 Access and Control

  • Data Access: You can view all your data within the app
  • Data Export: You can export your transaction data
  • Account Deletion: You can delete your account and all associated data

6.2 SMS Permissions

  • Permission Control: You can revoke SMS reading permissions at any time through device settings
  • Manual Entry: The app works without SMS permissions using manual transaction entry

6.3 Data Retention

  • Active Accounts: We retain your data as long as your account is active
  • Account Deletion: Data is permanently deleted within 30 days of account deletion
  • Legal Requirements: Some data may be retained longer if required by law

7. Children's Privacy

Findet is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

8. International Data Transfers

Your information may be transferred to and processed in countries other than your own. We ensure that such transfers comply with applicable data protection laws and provide adequate protection for your personal information.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy in the app
  • Sending you an email notification
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy

10. Third-Party Services

10.1 Google Firebase

We use Google Firebase for:

  • User authentication
  • Data storage and synchronization
  • Analytics and crash reporting
  • Push notifications

For more information about Google's privacy practices, please visit Google's Privacy Policy.

10.2 SMS Providers

The app reads SMS messages from banks and payment providers to automatically detect transactions. We do not share this SMS data with any third parties.

11. Data Subject Rights (GDPR)

If you are located in the European Economic Area, you have the following rights:

  • Right to Access: Request access to your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data
  • Right to Portability: Request transfer of your data
  • Right to Object: Object to processing of your data
  • Right to Restrict: Request restriction of processing

To exercise these rights, please contact us using the information below.

12. California Privacy Rights (CCPA)

If you are a California resident, you have the right to:

  • Know what personal information we collect and how it's used
  • Request deletion of your personal information
  • Opt-out of the sale of personal information (we do not sell your data)
  • Non-discrimination for exercising your privacy rights

13. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us:

Email: [findetlab.app@gmail.com]

Support: Visit our Support Page

For technical support within the app, you can also use the "Contact Support" feature in the app settings.

14. Definitions

  • Personal Information: Information that identifies, relates to, or could reasonably be linked with you
  • SMS Data: Text messages received on your device from banks and payment providers
  • Transaction Data: Financial transaction information including amounts, dates, and descriptions
  • Device Information: Information about your mobile device and how you use our app
